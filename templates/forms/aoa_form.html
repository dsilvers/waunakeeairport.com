Airport Operations Area (AOA) - Access Request

If you need access to your backyard by utilizing the airport property, please fill out the form below.

For immediate assistance, call or text Dan Silvers at (414) 416-0810 or Jim Meier at (608) 698-9498.

Access Guidelines

  • Avoid driving on the paved runway and the grass on the Woodland Dr. side. (this is a turf runway).
  • Vehicles should drive on the grass, staying close to the runway lights, on the Winston Way (South) side.
  • Turn on your Hazard lights.
  • Drive Slowly.
  • Exit your vehicle before entering the runway area to listen and look off both ends of the runway for aircraft landing or taking off.
  • Use a handheld radio to listen and announce your intentions. A radio is available for you to borrow.
  • Heavy equipment such as large trailers, campers, tree trimming equipment, and cement trucks can damage the runway and tear up the grass areas, especially in wet conditions.
  • For those wishing to access in the safety area on the East side of the airport, across from the airport and Division St. be especially aware of wet/soft turf conditions.
  • If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out to the contacts listed.
